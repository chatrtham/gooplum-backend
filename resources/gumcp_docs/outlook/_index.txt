3. **update_email** - Update email properties like read/unread status, flagged status, or move to folder

5. **forward_email** - Forward an email to specified recipients
6. **archive_email** - Archive an email by moving it to the Archive folder
7. **trash_email** - Move an email to the Trash/Deleted Items folder
8. **get_attachments** - Get attachments from an email. Downloads and uploads to Gumloop storage, returns filenames.

## Available Tools (5 total)

2. **search_and_filter_ads** - Search and filter ads by text content, date ranges, platforms, formats, niches, market targets, and languages. Leave query empty to filter without text search.

4. **get_brand_analytics** - Get comprehensive analytics data for a brand or Facebook page including running ads distribution and creative velocity over a date range.
5. **get_ad_details** - Get comprehensive details of a specific ad by its ID including metadata, creative content, targeting info, and performance data.
